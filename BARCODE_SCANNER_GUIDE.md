# 📱 Mobile Barcode Scanner Guide

## Overview
The POS system now supports using your phone's camera as a barcode scanner. This allows you to quickly add products to the cart by scanning their barcodes.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Access the POS System
1. Login as a seller
2. Navigate to the POS (Point of Sale) page
3. You'll see the barcode input field with a camera button (📸)

### Step 2: Start Scanning
1. Click the **camera button (📸)** next to the search field
2. Allow camera permissions when prompted by your browser
3. The camera scanner will open in a modal window

### Step 3: Scan Barcodes
1. Position the product barcode within the green frame on screen
2. Keep the barcode well-lit and steady
3. The scanner will automatically detect and read the barcode
4. You'll hear a beep sound when successful
5. The product will be automatically added to your cart

### Step 4: Continue Shopping
- The scanner closes automatically after a successful scan
- The product appears in your shopping cart
- You can click the camera button again to scan more items
- Or manually type/search for products using the text input

## Supported Barcode Types
The scanner supports multiple barcode formats:
- **EAN-13** (most common retail products)
- **EAN-8** (smaller products)
- **UPC-A/UPC-E** (US products)
- **Code 128** (shipping/logistics)
- **Code 39** (industrial)
- **Codabar** (libraries, blood banks)
- **Interleaved 2 of 5** (warehouse)

## Tips for Best Results

### Camera Position
- Hold your phone 10-20cm (4-8 inches) from the barcode
- Keep the camera parallel to the barcode surface
- Avoid extreme angles

### Lighting
- Ensure good lighting on the barcode
- Avoid shadows or glare on the barcode
- Use the phone's flashlight if needed in dark areas

### Barcode Condition
- Ensure barcodes are clean and not damaged
- Wrinkled or torn barcodes may be harder to scan
- If a barcode won't scan, try entering it manually

### Mobile vs Desktop
- **On Mobile Phones**: Uses the rear camera (best for scanning)
- **On Tablets**: Uses the rear camera
- **On Desktop/Laptop**: Uses the webcam (may be less reliable)

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
**Problem**: Camera doesn't start or shows permission error

**Solutions**:
1. Check browser permissions for camera access
2. Make sure no other app is using the camera
3. Try refreshing the page
4. On iOS: Use Safari browser (Chrome may have limitations)
5. On Android: Chrome or Firefox work best

### Barcode Not Detected
**Problem**: Scanner doesn't recognize the barcode

**Solutions**:
1. Move closer or further from the barcode
2. Improve lighting conditions
3. Hold the phone steadier
4. Try cleaning the barcode surface
5. Manually enter the barcode if scanning continues to fail

### Scanner Freezes
**Problem**: Camera view freezes or becomes unresponsive

**Solutions**:
1. Close the scanner modal and reopen it
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache and try again

## Browser Compatibility

### Recommended Browsers
- ✅ **Chrome/Chromium** (Android, Desktop)
- ✅ **Safari** (iOS, macOS)
- ✅ **Firefox** (Android, Desktop)
- ✅ **Edge** (Desktop)

### Mobile Devices
- **iOS 11+**: Works best in Safari
- **Android 5+**: Works in Chrome, Firefox
- Requires HTTPS connection (secure connection)

## Security & Privacy

### Camera Access
- Camera access is only requested when you click the camera button
- The camera feed is processed locally on your device
- No images or video are stored or transmitted
- You can revoke camera permissions at any time in browser settings

### HTTPS Required
- Camera access requires a secure HTTPS connection
- If using HTTP, the camera scanner won't work
- Use the manual text input as an alternative

## Alternative Input Methods

If the camera scanner doesn't work for you:

1. **Manual Entry**: Type the barcode number in the search field
2. **Product Search**: Search by product name instead
3. **USB Barcode Scanner**: Connect a traditional USB barcode scanner (acts as keyboard input)
4. **Bluetooth Scanner**: Pair a Bluetooth barcode scanner with your device

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Enter**: After typing a barcode, press Enter to search
- **Escape**: Close the camera scanner modal
- **Tab**: Navigate between input fields

## Performance Tips

### For Faster Scanning
1. Pre-focus the camera by tapping the barcode area
2. Use auto-focus if available in your device settings
3. Clean your camera lens regularly
4. Close unnecessary browser tabs to free up resources

### Battery Saving
- The camera scanner uses battery power
- Close the scanner when not actively scanning
- Consider using a wired/wireless charger during long shifts

## Training Tips for Sellers

1. **Practice First**: Try scanning a few test products before using in front of customers
2. **Have Backup**: Keep manual entry as a backup method
3. **Communicate**: Let customers know you're using a mobile scanner
4. **Speed**: After practice, scanning is faster than typing
5. **Accuracy**: Scanning reduces human error in barcode entry

## Contact Support

If you continue to experience issues:
1. Check your device's camera permissions
2. Update your browser to the latest version
3. Contact your system administrator
4. Report the issue with device/browser details
